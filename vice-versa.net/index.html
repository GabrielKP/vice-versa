Project


We encompasse the idea to create an app for constructive debating aimed at reducing polarization and enhancing understanding among society. In the past, we have noted a deterioration in the quality of debates, which to some extent have become less reflected, more polarized, and focused on convincing others rather than understanding the other's perspective. Therefore, our team is working on a platform that brings people together to debate controversial issues that are prevailing in our society.
